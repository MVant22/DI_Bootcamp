Wild & Wacky Vegetables

Kale, courgette, salsify, mustard, and broccoli blend with seakale, wakame, potato, fava bean, and tiger nut. Leek, celery, spinach, avocado, cabbage, celtuce, rutabaga, and kohlrabi create a rich medley. Parsnip, pea, soko, groundnut, and bitterleaf accompany spring onion.

Mung bean, quandong, kale, prairie turnip, celery, gram, zucchini, and pumpkin mix with green bean, silver beet, chard, endive, komatsuna, eggplant, kakadu plum, radish, and broccoli. Arugula, amaranth, fennel, wakame, peanut, and garlic enhance the blend. Sorrel, jicama, quandong, kale, chickpea, water chestnut, rock melon, and aubergine complement lettuce, pea, azuki bean, cress, onion, sea lettuce, dandelion, nori, and dulse rutabaga.
